Our Mission

For over 30 years, RiceTech Innovations has been at the forefront of the rice machinery industry. We are dedicated to engineering and manufacturing cutting-edge equipment that maximizes efficiency, reduces waste, and produces the highest quality rice. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for mills worldwide.

Our Machinery

Paddy Separators

Rice Whiteners

Advanced whiteners for producing polished and uniform rice grains.

Color Sorters

Optical sorting technology to remove impurities and defective grains.

Rice Graders

Precisely grades rice by size and shape for consistent quality.
